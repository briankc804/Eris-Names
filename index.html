AKAN NAMES GENERATOR

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
In Ghana, children are given their first name according to the day of the week they were born in.
This is a web application that takes a person's Birthday and calculates the day they were born in and generates their name according to their gender in Akan name

  Female

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama
